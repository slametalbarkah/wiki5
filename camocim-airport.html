Camocim Airport

Camocim is a municipality in the state of Ceará, Brazil, founded in 1879. The population is 63,907 people (2020 estimate) in an area of 1,120.45 km2 (432.61 sq mi).





== Transportation ==

Camocim is served by Camocim Airport.

Coronado Productions

Coronado Productions was a short lived film production company based in Britain in the 1950s. It was under the control of American David E. Rose and the films were originally distributed by RKO.

All its films in the 1950s had American directors. Many of its films involved an American visiting Europe or North Africa to solve a mystery from World War Two.

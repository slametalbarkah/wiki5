Xavier Mercier

Xavier Jacques Mercier (born 25 July 1989) is a French professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for GC Uchaud. He has previously represented Lesquin, Guingamp (for whom he made seven appearances in Ligue 2), Beauvais, KV Kortrijk, Cercle Brugge, OH Leuven, Ferencváros and RWDM.





== Career ==

In January 2008 he was called up for a training camp with the French under-21 futsal team for a 4-day camp.





=== Ferencváros ===

On 5 May 2023, he won the 2022–23 Nemzeti Bajnokság I with Ferencváros, after Kecskemét lost 1–0 to Honvéd at the Bozsik Aréna on the 30th matchday.

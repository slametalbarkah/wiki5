Ruda Maciejowska

Ruda Maciejowska [ˈruda mat͡ɕɛˈjɔfska] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Opole Lubelskie, within Opole Lubelskie County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 7 km (4 mi) east of Opole Lubelskie and 38 km (24 mi) west of the regional capital Lublin.

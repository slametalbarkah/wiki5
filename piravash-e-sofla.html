Piravash-e Sofla

Piravash-e Sofla (Persian: پيرواش سفلي, also Romanized as Pīrāvash-e Soflá; also known as Pīrāvash, Pīrāvash-e Jorjānīyeh, Pīrāvash-e Pā’īn, and Pīrāvosh) is a village in Aq Altin Rural District, in the Central District of Aqqala County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 4,692, in 1,012 families.

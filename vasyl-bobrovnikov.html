Vasyl Bobrovnikov

Vasyl Valentinovich Bobrovnikov (Ukrainian: Василь Валентинович Бобровников, born 8 November 1971) is a Ukrainian retired professional ice hockey player. He mainly played for Sokil Kyiv in Ukraine. He played internationally for the Ukrainian national team at several World Championships, as well as the 2002 Winter Olympics.

Ziaabad, Komijan

Ziaabad (Persian: ضيااباد, also Romanized as Ẕīā’ābād, Zeyā’ābād, and Zīā’ābād; also known as Ziādābād) is a village in Khenejin Rural District, in the Central District of Komijan County, Markazi Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 339, in 78 families.

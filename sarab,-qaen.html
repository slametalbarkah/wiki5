Sarab, Qaen

Sarab (Persian: سراب, also Romanized as Sarāb; also known as Sūrab) is a village in Afriz Rural District, Sedeh District, Qaen County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 185, in 46 families.

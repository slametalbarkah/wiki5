Zouleiha Dabonne

Zoulehia Abzetta Dabonne (born 15 December 1992) is an Ivory Coast judoka. At the 2016 Summer Olympics she competed in the Women's ‍–‍57 kg. At the 2020 Summer Olympics, she competed in the women's 57 kg event.

In 2021, Dabonne won one of the bronze medals in her event at the 2021 African Judo Championships held in Dakar, Senegal.

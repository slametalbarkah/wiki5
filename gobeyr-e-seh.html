Gobeyr-e Seh

Gobeyr-e Seh (Persian: گبيرسه; also known as Gobairé Sehe Shaikh Shaye, Gobeyr, and Gobeyr-e Seh-e Sheykh Shāyeh) is a village in Anaqcheh Rural District, in the Central District of Ahvaz County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,024, in 215 families.

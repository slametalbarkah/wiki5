Mian Mil

Mian Mil (Persian: ميان ميل, also Romanized as Mīān Mīl, Meyān Mīl, and Mīānmīl) is a village in Howmeh-ye Sarpol Rural District, in the Central District of Sarpol-e Zahab County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 127, in 26 families.

Rick Trickett

Rick Trickett is an American college football coach. He is the offensive line coach for the Jacksonville State Gamecocks and a former American football player. He served as the head football coach at his alma mater, Glenville State College in Glenville, West Virginia, in 1999. Trickett worked as an offensive line coach at a number of colleges, notably at Florida State University under Jimbo Fisher.

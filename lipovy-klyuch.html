Lipovy Klyuch

Lipovy Klyuch (Russian: Липовый Ключ; Bashkir: Йүкәле Шишмә, Yükäle Şişmä) is a rural locality (a village) in Verkhnebishindinsky Selsoviet, Tuymazinsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 84 as of 2010. There are 2 streets.





== Geography ==

Lipovy Klyuch is located 18 km southeast of Tuymazy (the district's administrative centre) by road. Nizhniye Bishindy is the nearest rural locality.

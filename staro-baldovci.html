Staro Baldovci

Staro Baldovci (Macedonian: Старо Балдовци) is a village in the municipality of Bosilovo, North Macedonia.





== Demographics ==

As of the 2021 census, Staro Baldovci had 422 residents with the following ethnic composition:



Turks 222

Persons for whom data are taken from administrative sources 109

Macedonians 90

Roma 1

According to the 2002 census, the village had a total of 269 inhabitants.

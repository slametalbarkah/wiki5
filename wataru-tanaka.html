Wataru Tanaka

Wataru Tanaka (田中 渉, Tanaka Wataru, born 23 September 2000) is a Japanese footballer currently playing as a midfielder for Kagoshima United on loan from Montedio Yamagata.





== Personal life ==

Tanaka is the brother of fellow footballer Hiromu Tanaka.

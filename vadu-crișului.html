Vadu Crișului

Vadu Crișului (Hungarian: Rév) is a commune in Bihor County, Crișana, Romania with a population of 4,009 people.





== Populated places ==

It is composed of four villages: Birtin (Bertény), Topa de Criș (Köröstopa), Tomnatic (Tomnatek) and Vadu Crișului.
